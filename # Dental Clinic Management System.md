# Dental Clinic Management System
A Python-based registration and financial reporting system for a dental practice.

## Features
- **Client Registration:** Capture ID, Name, and Appointment type.
- **Dynamic Pricing:** Automatic cost calculation based on client insurance (Particular, EPS, Prepaid).
- **Business Logic:** Handles specific rules for service quantities (Cleanings vs. Extractions).
- **Automated Reporting:** - Total revenue calculation.
  - Extraction service counter.
  - Descending revenue sort (Highest to Lowest).
